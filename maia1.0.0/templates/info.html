About MAIA

MAIA (Medical AI Assistant) is a specialized AI system designed to assist with medical image analysis, including brain MRI tumor detection, classification, Alzheimer's impairment identification, and COVID-19 detection from medical images.

The system uses several trained neural network models optimized for the NVIDIA Jetson platform, delivering fast and accurate predictions to support medical professionals.

You can upload your MRI images on the main page and select the analysis mode. MAIA will process the image and provide a prediction with confidence scores.
